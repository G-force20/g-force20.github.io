Students with State-Issued Disability Placards:

If you have a State-Issued Disability Placard, you do not need to contact Disability Support Services. Instead, simply call (610)758-7275 or email inpark@lehigh.edu to apply for a permit. You do not need to fill out any forms through Disability Support Services.

You will need to send a copy of your state issued disability permit, placard, or plate to the Parking office to inpark@lehigh.edu.

To request AccessLU transportation services, simply call (610)758-4410 to request on-demand service.

Then, you will need to download and set up the TransLoc application to request rides.
